Maggie Servais

An accomplished clothing designer, Maggie Servais brings a stylist's eye to event and lifestyle management in the San Francisco Bay Area. She's a hands-on collaborator who delivers on aesthetics and efficiency. Recently, Maggie designed and built Oakland's Coloso Coffee. The bright Uptown space showcases her approach to blending salvaged finds with modern beauty. Maggie has a passion for residential projects and uses her spare time to create repurposed planters out of found materials. She holds a BFA from California College of the Arts.

Jessie Galloway

Jessie Galloway draws on her background in interior architecture to provide design and organizational services in the San Francisco Bay Area. From home staging to furnishing, she understands how people interact with the structural boundaries of their spaces and creates an adaptable experience for residential and commercial clientele. A tastemaker who takes pleasure in bringing simplicity and style to everyday life, Jessie is passionate about pleasing her clients and consults on trend and design directions in a variety of categories. She holds an MFA from Academy of Art University.
